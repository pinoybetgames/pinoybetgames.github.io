PinoyBetGames Goes Live on FOMO.io

PinoyBetGames, a rising star in localized iGaming content, officially goes live with FOMO.io, a leading next-gen crypto gaming platform.

This strategic integration brings PinoyBetGames’ unique suite of Filipino-inspired titles — including real-time betting games and reimagined local favorites — to the growing global audience of FOMO.io, further strengthening the platform’s content diversity and localization strategy.

“We’re excited to launch with FOMO.io, a platform that’s redefining the iGaming space through innovation and a strong focus on player engagement,” said Bert C., CEO of PinoyBetGames. “Our goal has always been to showcase localized creativity to the world, and this partnership brings us one step closer.”

FOMO.io, known for blending blockchain tech with interactive gaming, sees the launch as part of its broader effort to deliver fresh, culturally rich content to its expanding user base.

“PinoyBetGames offers something truly different — games that tell a story and reflect a culture,” said Jasmine Ng, Head of Content Partnerships at FOMO.io. “We’re thrilled to bring these titles to our platform and look forward to seeing how our community embraces them.”

The games are now available to FOMO.io users, offering a mix of quick-play experiences and social betting mechanics that appeal to both casual and seasoned players. With this launch, both companies are signaling their intent to innovate and expand in the fast-growing world of crypto-enabled gaming.
