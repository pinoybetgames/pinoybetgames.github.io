The Legend Lives On

For decades, treasure hunters have searched the Philippine jungles for Yamashita’s Gold—the legendary hoard of war loot supposedly buried by Japanese General Yamashita at the end of World War II. Yamashita’s Treasure turns this enduring legend into a high-stakes adventure where every spin brings you closer to unimaginable riches.

Dense jungles, hidden caves, ancient maps, and the constant threat of booby traps—this game has it all.

Dig for Glory

Treasure Map Bonus Trail

Land three map symbols and begin your expedition. Navigate through the jungle, making choices at each fork in the path. Some routes lead to treasure. Others lead to traps. The deeper you go, the greater the rewards—if you survive.

Golden Buddha Jackpot

The legendary Golden Buddha sits at the heart of the treasure. This progressive jackpot grows with every spin across all players. Trigger the jackpot round by landing five golden Buddha symbols, then pick your excavation site for a chance at the ultimate prize.

Cave Exploration Free Spins

Enter the cave system for free spins in darkness. Your torch illuminates only part of the reels, adding mystery to each spin. Find additional torch symbols to reveal more of the grid and increase your chances of massive combinations.

Booby Trap Risk Feature

After any base game win, choose to take the risk. Attempt to bypass the booby trap and double your winnings—or trigger the trap and lose it all. High risk, high reward, pure adrenaline.

Progressive Treasure Meter

Every gold symbol that lands fills the treasure meter. When it’s full, you’re guaranteed entry to the bonus trail on your next spin. It’s a goal to chase that keeps players engaged through dry spells.

Adventure Awaits

Yamashita’s Treasure is built for the thrill-seekers. High volatility means the wins don’t come easy, but when they do, they’re worth the journey. The expanded 5x4 grid with 50 paylines offers plenty of action, while the adventure theme appeals to players who want more than just spinning symbols.

  • Legendary story that captivates imaginations
  • High volatility for experienced players
  • Multiple bonus paths add strategic depth
  • Cinematic presentation worthy of the adventure
