The King of Philippine Roads

Nothing says Philippines quite like the Jeepney—the colorful, chrome-decorated, music-blasting king of public transport. Jeepney Scatter celebrates this cultural icon with a game that’s as vibrant, chaotic, and rewarding as a rush-hour ride through Manila.

From the hand-painted artwork to the authentic sound of jeepney horns, this game is a love letter to Filipino street culture.

Features That Keep Rolling

Route Bonus Free Spins

Land three jeepney symbols and choose your route. Different routes offer different risk/reward profiles: the short Makati route for guaranteed smaller wins, or the long Divisoria route for volatile big-win potential. Your choice, your adventure.

Passenger Pick Feature

When the passenger symbol appears, pick up riders along your route. Each passenger holds a hidden prize—cash, multipliers, or access to additional features. The jeepney can hold up to 20 passengers (just like real life), so keep picking until it’s full.

Scatter Pays

True to the game’s name, scatter symbols pay in any position. No paylines required. Just land enough scatters anywhere on the reels and collect your fare.

Jeepney Wild Expansion

The Jeepney Wild doesn’t just substitute—it expands. When it lands, it can stretch to fill its entire reel, turning a modest win into something much bigger. Watch for it on the center reels where it has the most impact.

Traffic Jam Respins

Sometimes the jeepney gets stuck in traffic. When specific symbol combinations land, the reels lock and you get free respins on the remaining positions. Frustrating in real life, profitable in the game.

Authentically Filipino

Jeepney Scatter works because it’s genuine. The artwork was created by Filipino artists. The sound design captures real Manila streets. The gameplay mechanics reflect the unpredictable, anything-goes nature of jeepney travel.

For Filipino players, it’s instant nostalgia. For everyone else, it’s an introduction to one of the world’s most unique cultural icons.
