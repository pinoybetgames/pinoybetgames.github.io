In a bold move set to enhance the online gaming landscape across Southeast Asia, DigitalWin, a leading technology and content distribution platform, has officially entered into a strategic partnership with PinoyBetGames, a fast-growing Filipino iGaming content provider.

The partnership, sealed on May 16, 2025, marks a significant milestone for both companies as they aim to deliver localized, innovative, and engaging gaming experiences to a broader player base. Through this collaboration, PinoyBetGames’ popular portfolio—featuring Filipino-themed games such as Sari-Sari Store, Jeepney Scatter , and Color Game—will now be integrated into DigitalWin’s expansive network of partner operators and platforms.

“We are thrilled to partner with PinoyBetGames, whose commitment to culturally relevant and high-quality content aligns perfectly with our mission to deliver next-generation iGaming solutions,” said Gary S., Managing Director of DigitalWin. “This partnership will strengthen our regional presence and allow us to offer more diverse content that truly resonates with the Asian market.”

PinoyBetGames has gained traction in recent years for its focus on creating engaging titles tailored for Filipino players, blending traditional gameplay with modern digital formats. This collaboration is expected to amplify their visibility while giving operators within DigitalWin’s network access to uniquely localized content that stands out in a competitive market.

“Partnering with DigitalWin is a major leap forward for us,” said Bert C., CEO at PinoyBetGames. “This alliance opens up exciting distribution opportunities and accelerates our goal of becoming a leading provider of Filipino-made iGaming content across Asia.”

With this partnership, both companies are poised to not only increase their market share but also contribute to the growth of homegrown digital entertainment in the region. The integration process is already underway, with several games from PinoyBetGames expected to go live across DigitalWin’s partner platforms in the coming weeks.
