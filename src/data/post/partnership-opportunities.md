## We're Open for Business

After months of development and testing, we're excited to announce that **Pinoy Bet Games is now accepting partnership inquiries** from operators, aggregators, and platforms worldwide.

If you're looking to expand your game portfolio with high-quality, culturally relevant titles designed for Asian markets, we want to hear from you.

## What We Offer

### Ready-Made Games

Our portfolio of nine HTML5 games is production-ready. No waiting for development cycles. No beta-quality builds. What you see is what goes live.

### Fast Integration

We've designed our games for minimal integration friction. Standard APIs, clear documentation, and direct technical support from the team that built them.

### Transparent Terms

No hidden fees. No surprise revenue shares. We believe partnerships work best when both sides understand exactly what they're getting into.

### Ongoing Support

Integration isn't the end of the relationship—it's the beginning. We provide continuous support, regular updates, and collaborative problem-solving.

## Partnership Models

We offer flexible partnership structures to match your needs:

### Direct Integration

For operators with technical capacity, integrate our games directly into your platform. We provide APIs, documentation, and hands-on support.

### Aggregator Partnerships

Already working with a game aggregator? We're open to aggregator partnerships that bring our games to multiple platforms efficiently.

### White Label Options

For select partners, we offer white-label arrangements with customization options for branding and configuration.

## Who We're Looking For

The best partnerships are built on alignment. We work best with partners who:

- **Focus on Asian markets** — particularly Southeast Asia, where our cultural themes resonate most strongly
- **Value quality over quantity** — partners who care about player experience, not just lobby count
- **Communicate directly** — we prefer straightforward discussions over formal RFP processes
- **Think long-term** — we're building relationships, not just signing contracts

## How to Get Started

Ready to explore a partnership? Here's how it works:

1. **Reach out** — [Contact us](/contact) with basic information about your platform and market focus
2. **Discovery call** — We'll schedule a conversation to understand your needs and share our capabilities
3. **Demo access** — Get hands-on with our games in a test environment
4. **Commercial discussion** — If there's a fit, we'll work out terms that make sense for both sides
5. **Integration support** — Our team works alongside yours to ensure smooth integration

## Let's Talk

The Asian gaming market is evolving rapidly. Players want games that feel authentic. Operators want partners they can trust. We built PBG to deliver both.

If that sounds like what you're looking for, [get in touch](/contact). We're excited to hear from you.
