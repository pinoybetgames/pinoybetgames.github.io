In a move aimed at accelerating global growth and content distribution, PinoyBetGames, a fast-emerging iGaming content provider known for Filipino-themed games, has officially partnered with ReelSoft, a leading gaming platform and technology provider.

The partnership will see ReelSoft powering the distribution of PinoyBetGames’ portfolio through its advanced aggregation platform, giving operators seamless access to localized, culturally resonant content that has already gained traction in Southeast Asia and diaspora markets.

“This is a significant milestone for us,” said Bert C., CEO of PinoyBetGames. “ReelSoft’s proven technology and global network align perfectly with our mission to bring uniquely Filipino games to a wider international audience. Through this partnership, we can scale faster while maintaining our focus on innovation and cultural authenticity.”

ReelSoft, which has a strong track record of onboarding and distributing content for next-gen operators, emphasized its enthusiasm for working with emerging studios that deliver niche and standout experiences.

“We’re proud to welcome PinoyBetGames to the ReelSoft family,” said Thomas N., CEO at ReelSoft. “Their localized content fills a gap in the market and offers a refreshing alternative to mainstream titles. We’re excited to support their growth and introduce their games to new markets through our platform.”

This collaboration enables operators connected to ReelSoft to offer PinoyBetGames’ real-time betting content, arcade-style games, and reimagined local classics directly through a single integration. The partnership also paves the way for future co-development initiatives and deeper platform enhancements.

As demand continues to rise for culturally specific content that connects with local audiences, the ReelSoft–PinoyBetGames partnership is poised to deliver compelling, differentiated entertainment to players around the globe.
