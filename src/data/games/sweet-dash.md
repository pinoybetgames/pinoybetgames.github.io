## Race through the candy circuit

Sweet Dash is a fast-paced, arcade-style slot where vibrant candy icons, neon tracks and lightning-fast animations combine for high-energy play. Spins are quick, cascades are frequent, and the game rewards streaks with growing multipliers.

## Core mechanics

### Dash Meter

Every cascade or win fills the Dash Meter. When full, the Dash Meter triggers a Turbo Respin sequence with higher chances of Sticky Wilds and an active win multiplier.

### Candy Dash Free Spins

Landing 3 or more Scatter symbols awards Free Spins. During Free Spins multipliers increase with each consecutive cascade, and select wilds become sticky for the remainder of the round.

### Cascading Wins & Multipliers

Winning symbols disappear and are replaced by new ones, creating cascades. Each successful cascade increases the active multiplier (reset on non-winning spins), amplifying long win chains.

### Sticky Wilds & Respins

Sticky Wilds can lock in place for multiple spins during bonus rounds. Combined with the Dash Meter, Sticky Wilds often lead to extended respin sequences and big-concept wins.
