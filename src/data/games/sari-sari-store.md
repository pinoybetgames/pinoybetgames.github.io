## Every Neighborhood Has One

The sari-sari store is the heart of Filipino community life—a tiny shop selling everything from soap to snacks, run by the neighborhood "tinderas" who know everyone by name. **Sari-Sari Store** captures this warm, familiar experience in a game that feels like coming home.

From the cluttered shelves to the hanging sachets, every detail has been crafted with authentic love for this uniquely Filipino institution.

## Homegrown Features

### Tindahan Bonus Round

Become the storekeeper. Customers arrive at your window with requests—fulfill their orders correctly and collect tips. Miss an item and they might go to the competitor down the street. It's a skill-based bonus that rewards attention.

### Suki Customer Feature

Regular customers (sukis) randomly appear during base play, bringing guaranteed wins. The more you play, the more sukis you attract, and the better their rewards become. It's a loyalty system built right into the game.

### Stock Up Multipliers

Every winning spin adds stock to your shelves. When the shelves are full, you trigger a multiplied payout on your next win. It's a visible, satisfying progression that gives meaning to smaller wins.

### Neighborhood Jackpot

Connected across all Sari-Sari Store players, the Neighborhood Jackpot grows with every spin. Random triggers give any player a chance to win—because in a real neighborhood, fortune can knock on any door.

### Utang Collection Mini-Game

In a playful nod to Filipino culture, collect outstanding debts (utang) from customers. Pick which neighbors to visit, and hope they're home with money. A humorous feature that resonates deeply with local players.
