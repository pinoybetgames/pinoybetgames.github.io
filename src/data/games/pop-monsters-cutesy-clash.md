## How the Monsters Play

### Cluster Pays

Forget paylines. In Pop Monsters, wins happen when 5 or more matching monsters connect horizontally or vertically. The bigger the cluster, the bigger the payout. Clusters of 15+ monsters trigger special bonus multipliers.

### Cascade Wins

When monsters form a winning cluster, they pop and disappear, making room for new monsters to fall from above. If those new monsters form another cluster, they pop too. Chains can continue indefinitely, with multipliers increasing with each cascade.

### Monster Evolution

Each monster has three evolution stages. When you collect enough of one monster type during a session, it evolves into a stronger form worth more on the paytable. Keep playing to power up your monsters and maximize your wins.

### Cutesy Clash Bonus

Trigger the bonus when two rival monster types appear in large numbers on the same spin. Watch them battle in an animated clash that determines your bonus prize. The cuter the monster, the fiercer the fighter.

### Collection Album

Collect all 12 monster types to complete your album and unlock the secret 13th monster—a rare symbol worth up to 500x your bet on its own.
